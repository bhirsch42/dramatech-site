"The 39 Steps" is Now Open!

COME ONE, COME ALL!

DramaTech is currently presenting "The 39 Steps"! There are 5 performances left (check under the "shows" tab for more details). In addition, there are free LTT! improv shows following the performance on Saturday the 5th and Friday the 11th.

Ticket prices are listed below.

Students with valid student ID: $5

General: $10

Faculty: $8

And, just to clear any confusion, students include not only Georgia Tech students, but any student with a valid student ID.

Come have fun with us! Enjoy the night and (maybe) find out the secret of the 39 Steps!
